# Market Positioning & Go-To-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

Settler Protocol is positioned as the **open-source alternative** to proprietary financial reconciliation platforms, with a clear path to enterprise-grade managed services. Our strategy focuses on developer adoption through free, self-hostable OSS, with natural upgrade paths to enterprise features.

---

## Market Positioning

### Primary Positioning

**"The Open-Source Protocol for Financial Reconciliation"**

- **Target Audience**: Developers, engineering teams, fintech companies
- **Value Proposition**: Free, self-hostable, no vendor lock-in
- **Differentiator**: Only open-source reconciliation protocol with enterprise upgrade path

### Secondary Positioning

**"Enterprise-Grade Reconciliation, Open-Source First"**

- **Target Audience**: Enterprises evaluating reconciliation solutions
- **Value Proposition**: Start free, scale to enterprise when needed
- **Differentiator**: No lock-in, can self-host or use managed services
